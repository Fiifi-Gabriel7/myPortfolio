About Me

I'm a passionate software engineer with growing expertise in full-stack development and a strong background in Iron works (steel bending). With over 5 years of hands-on construction experience. I bridge the gap between physical structures and digital solutions. I’m currently enrolled at Codetrain Africa, training to become a full-stack software engineer. I’m on a mission to build efficient, real-world applications while sharpening my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Java, React and version control with Git & GitHub, and further to backend development. Based in Ghana, coding my way into the future by night because time is a burden.
